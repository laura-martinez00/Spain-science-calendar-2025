    Disseminating and Researching, Mission Impossible?
    Two well-known Spanish science communicators will discuss their experience balancing active scientific research with science communication. The speakers are Héctor Socas Navarro, physicist at the Institute of Astrophysics of the Canary Islands and director of the successful science podcast "Coffee Break," and Conchi Lillo, neuroscientist at the Institute of Neuroscience of Castile and León at the University of Salamanca, and science communicator on social media and author of popular science books.
